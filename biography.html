1995

I was born July 10th, 1995 in Overland Park, Kansas. I had two parents, a brother, and a sister. My parents owned a small landscaping and concrete business. I would help there often during the summer months and weekends. I played soccer, wrestled, and played baseball.

2013

I graduated High school from Basehor-Linwood High school in 2013. All four years of highschool I was apart of the soccer, powerlifting, and baseball teams. I also played in our school band and Jazz Band. While I didn't know what I wanted to do after high school, I attended a local community college, Kansas City Kansas Community College for a semester while on a band scholarship. Shortly after I decided to transfer to the University of Kansas.

2017

I ended up dropping out of the University of Kansas in 2016 and felt lost. I decided to take a break from school and search for a purpose. On June 1 2017, I joined the United States Navy where my job was to be a Hospital Corpsman. From there I ended up being stationed in San Antonio,TX, San Diego, CA, and Twentynine Palms, CA. Being from Kansas and joining the Navy I was hoping to be near water but alas I was sent to the desert after my time in San Diego.

2021

After getting out of the Navy in 2021, and my then girlfriend at the time, Danielle, returning from a 10 month deployment, we decided to get married! We got married on September 3,2021. In 2022, my wife would also leave the navy and we would move to Orlando,FL for 6 months for a new start, but decided that it wasn't the place for us. So we moved to where my wife was from Minneapolis, MN. Here we would buy our first home together in 2023 where we have our two dogs, Cece and Bonnie, and two cats, Bruce and Nori. And in 2023 we both started attending University at St.Thomas and St. Kate's respectively.
